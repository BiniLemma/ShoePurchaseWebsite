Welcome to Addis Shoe

Addis Shoe is an Ethiopian multinational corporation that is engaged in the distribution, transport, marketing and sales of footwear worldwide . The company is headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It is the countries largest supplier of athletic and non-athletic shoes. As of 2020, it employed 3,000 people nationwide. In 2020 the company has made an immense profit even in the midst of a worldwide pandemic, making it the most valuable brand among shoe businesses.
